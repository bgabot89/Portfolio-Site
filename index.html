About Me

My name is Brian Gabot. I am a developer currently seeking a full-stack/or Front-end developer position. I graduated from the University of California, Santa Cruz in 2011 and had plans to become an accountant or financial analyst. However, I felt that these job occupations would not make me happy. I continued to work a few jobs dealing with business operations and analytics. One particular task that I was assigned as a catalog coordinator at Glyde was to manage website content. It was then I realized that my true passion was to create things that people would find useful, it was here on out that I decided to make a change into a web development career. In order to achieve my goal, I attended General Assembly's Web Development Immersive Program in September 2015 and graduated in December 2015.

While I'm not in front of my computer developing or illustrating, I like to play table-top or video games.
